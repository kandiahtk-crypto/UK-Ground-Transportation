Modern Slavery Statement

This statement is made pursuant to Section 54 of the UK Modern Slavery Act 2015 and outlines the steps taken by Evershine Transport Limited to prevent modern slavery and human trafficking within its operations and supply chain.

Zero-tolerance approach Ethical operations Supplier due diligence Ongoing review
Business profile

Our business

Evershine Transport Limited provides trade-only inbound ground transport services for tour operators and DMCs across the United Kingdom and Ireland. Our work includes vehicle coordination, itinerary planning and regulated transport delivery.

Commitment

Our commitment

We are committed to conducting business ethically and with integrity. We operate a zero-tolerance approach to modern slavery, forced labour and human trafficking within our organisation and supply chain.

Supply chain

Supply chain & due diligence

Our supply chain primarily consists of regulated transport providers and associated operational partners. We expect suppliers to comply with applicable UK legislation and to uphold fair labour practices.

  • Engagement with regulated and compliant service providers
  • Expectation of lawful employment practices
  • Right to review and terminate relationships in cases of non-compliance
Risk management

Policies & risk management

While the nature of our operations presents a relatively low risk of modern slavery, we remain vigilant and committed to reviewing operational and supplier relationships where appropriate.

Review

Ongoing review

This statement is reviewed periodically to support continued compliance with the Modern Slavery Act 2015 and to reflect any material changes to our operations or supplier framework.
