Vehicle categories

Fleet

Vehicle selection should support the operational needs of your programme: passenger comfort, luggage capacity, route suitability, and presentation standards. The categories below outline typical options requested by tour operators and DMCs.

Mini coach

Appropriate for smaller groups and tighter access requirements.

  • Best for: small touring groups, executive movements
  • Considerations: luggage capacity depends on model
  • Suitable for: city access where larger vehicles are restricted

Midi coach

Balanced option for mid-size groups and regional touring.

  • Best for: day excursions and mid-size touring programmes
  • Considerations: confirm luggage and seating requirements
  • Suitable for: mixed routing across cities and regions

Full-size coach

Standard solution for larger groups and multi-day itineraries.

  • Best for: series departures and group touring
  • Considerations: hotel access and loading logistics
  • Suitable for: long-distance routing and touring programmes

Executive / premium specification

Used where presentation and comfort expectations are elevated (VIP, premium inbound, corporate and incentive groups).

  • Best for: premium private groups and executive movements
  • Considerations: onboard features vary by specification
  • Confirm requirements: seating style, storage, accessibility

Accessibility requirements

Where programmes require accessible transport, suitability should be confirmed at quotation stage.

  • Please specify accessibility needs in the enquiry
  • Include passenger numbers and mobility requirements
  • Allow additional time for boarding and logistics

Fleet standards and presentation

Vehicle suitability is assessed against passenger numbers, luggage volume, route profile and access requirements. Presentation and cleanliness are maintained to professional standards appropriate for international inbound groups.

Operational suitability

Vehicle allocation is aligned to route feasibility and programme timings, including city access constraints where applicable.
