Fleet overview

Vehicles for inbound programmes

Final vehicle allocation is confirmed at quotation stage, based on routing detail, luggage estimates, guide seat requirements and any accessibility considerations.

Mini Coach

Suitable for smaller groups, city movements and regional excursions where access is restricted.

  • Ideal for tighter city access and smaller loading zones
  • Capacity planning confirmed by programme detail
  • Luggage volume must be stated for accurate allocation

Midi Coach

Balanced option for medium group sizes, touring programmes and mixed city/regional routing.

  • Suitable for multi-day touring with moderate luggage loads
  • Good operational balance between access and capacity
  • Recommended when programme includes varied regions

Full-size Coach

Preferred for larger groups and contracted touring programmes with full luggage requirements.

  • Best for airport transfers and larger touring groups
  • Feasibility must account for access limits and set-down rules
  • Driver hours + touring structure reviewed at planning stage

Executive specification

Executive presentation options are available for MICE movements, VIP groups and premium touring programmes. Specification is confirmed in the quotation with clear inclusions.

  • Defined vehicle standard and presentation expectations
  • Professional operational control for time-sensitive movements
  • Suitable for conference schedules and contracted service levels

Accessibility considerations

If accessibility requirements apply, include details early so feasibility and vehicle suitability can be confirmed.

  • Declare mobility / assistance requirements in advance
  • Confirm boarding needs and passenger handling expectations
  • Routing feasibility reviewed for realistic timings
Allocation criteria

What we need to allocate correctly

UK touring programmes are influenced by luggage volume, day-by-day routing, and access constraints at hotels and attractions. To avoid mismatch on the day, please include the following at enquiry stage:

Passenger and luggage detail

  • Exact passenger count (and guide seat requirement)
  • Luggage estimate (suitcases + carry-ons)
  • Any oversize items (equipment, cases, samples)

Programme structure

  • Pick-up / drop-off locations (postcodes where possible)
  • Multi-day outline with hotel names (if known)
  • Fixed time windows (attractions, flights, appointments)
Trade enquiries

Confirm vehicle suitability for your programme

Send dates, passenger numbers, routing and luggage estimate. We will review feasibility and return a clear quotation with defined specification.
