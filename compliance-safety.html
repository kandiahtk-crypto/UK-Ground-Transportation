Compliance framework

Compliance & Safety

Transport delivery must meet professional and regulatory standards aligned with UK legislation. Operational discipline, insurance coverage, and vehicle maintenance procedures are integral to service delivery.

Regulatory compliance

Operations are conducted in accordance with UK transport legislation, including driver hours regulations and vehicle operating standards. Route planning and scheduling consider compliance from the outset.

Insurance coverage

Appropriate insurance policies are maintained in line with UK regulatory requirements. Confirmation of cover can be provided during contracting or compliance review processes.

Vehicle maintenance

Vehicles are subject to structured maintenance schedules and safety inspections to ensure operational reliability and passenger safety.

Driver standards

Drivers are professionally presented and briefed prior to service. Where required, documentation or verification procedures can be provided for specific programme needs.

Risk awareness

Itinerary review includes access considerations, timing feasibility and contingency awareness to reduce operational disruption.

Documentation

Supporting documentation may be provided where required for contracting, venue requirements or programme risk assessment.
