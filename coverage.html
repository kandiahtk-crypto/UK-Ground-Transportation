Coordination

UK-wide coordination

Coordination is structured for both single movements and multi-day touring programmes. For itinerary planning, postcode-level pickup and drop-off detail is recommended.

Typical inbound routing requirements

  • London arrivals and departures (airports, hotels, venues)
  • London → Oxford / Cambridge / Bath / Stonehenge
  • London → Cotswolds → Stratford-upon-Avon
  • Manchester / Liverpool movements and touring
  • Scotland touring programmes (including multi-city routing)
  • Wales touring programmes and regional excursions

Planning notes

  • Access constraints and set-down rules vary by city and venue.
  • Driver hours compliance must be reflected in touring structure.
  • Clear timings reduce on-the-day disruption for groups.
