Planning considerations (important for inbound groups)

UK itineraries are influenced by access restrictions, congestion patterns, venue time windows and driver hours compliance. To support accurate planning and pricing, please provide the following where possible:

  • Postcodes / precise locations (hotels, venues, attractions)
  • Passenger numbers and luggage estimate
  • Timing expectations and any fixed appointment times
  • Number of tour days and daily outline for multi-day programmes
  • Accessibility requirements (if applicable)

A structured review is then completed to confirm operational feasibility before final quotation.
